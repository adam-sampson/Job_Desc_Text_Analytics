The abstractions will be optimally assembled into data processing and machine learning pipelines in an easy to use manner by developers and end business users. This framework should make Big-Data Technology agnostic to the users, so users can concentrate on the business logic. The framework will have a clear distinction about the cold data, warm data and hot data, and the framework, while serving the user with a unified interface, has the ability to move the data behind the scene transparently.
