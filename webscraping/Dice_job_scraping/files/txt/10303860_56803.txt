Position Description : The Lead Data Engineer is responsible for the leading and development of complex data integration processes, data transformation, and developing advanced visualizations within Penn Medicine. This individual will be required to ensure that the extracted, transformed, and displayed information meets all clinical and business requirements of the organization and end-users. This will entail creating new as well as maintaining and modifying existing development. This individual will be required to provide technical counsel, guidance, and technical leadership to other team members, consultants, IS Staff, and end-users within their core operating area. It is expected that this person will mentor and assist in the growth of more junior team members. This individual will be responsible for creating and maintaining accurate project and process documentation. This position will be required to lead projects and work with other key IS stakeholders and team leaders to coordinate necessary resources, outline action plans, and make final development decisions.
