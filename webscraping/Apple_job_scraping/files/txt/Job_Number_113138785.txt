 iPhone HW - Prototyping &amp; Tools Team Engineer Job Number: 113138785 Santa Clara Valley, California, United States Posted: Oct. 24, 2017 Weekly Hours: 40.00 Job Summary Apple’s iPhone Hardware Engineering organization is looking for a high-energy engineer with a real passion for hardware and software.
