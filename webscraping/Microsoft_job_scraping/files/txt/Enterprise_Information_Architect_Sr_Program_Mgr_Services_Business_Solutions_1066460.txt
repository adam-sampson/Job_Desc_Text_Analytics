The Enterprise Information Architect / Sr Program Manager is a foundational role to provide solid information architecture expertise across our worldwide Services business and data systems – on the Data Management team. You will work across a highly matrixed organization with cross-functional teams across the Services organization to provide architecture leadership to support Services business stakeholders. You, as a technical leader, support several mission critical Microsoft Services initiatives with the goal of providing information architecture / data architecture expertise across the business and data platforms for both the current and future state architecture. You will be responsible for data management roadmap for information architecture for data tools/systems including MDM, metadata, modeling and other tools and systems, as well as alignment with business capabilities and strategic project enablement. Also, you are responsible for alignment with key stakeholders and peers – such as Data Governance, MDM, BI/Insights, Business Systems process teams, IT and engineering counterparts and Services business leaders. You will lead integration of complex initiatives pertaining to our business and data architectures to ensure successful implementation, execution and support.
