Are you interested in uncovering business insights from big data? Are you a professional in performing data sciences to solve real world problems? If so, then we have a unique opportunity for you. Microsoft’s Universal Store Business Operations Analytics team is looking for a data scientist who is passionate about online commerce and is looking to contribute enabling ecommerce capabilities for all of Microsoft’s consumer and business online services such as Windows Store, Microsoft Store, XBOX, Outlook, Office 365, Azure, Bing, and Dynamics, to just name a few. As a right candidate, you are a customer-focused, detail-oriented, results-driven analytic professional with great communication skills and a strong knowledge of predictive modeling and statistical techniques in the payments and fraud prevention space. Manipulate large volumes of data, create new and improved techniques and/or solutions for data collection, management and usage. 3. Conduct experiments to gain insights into the business performance and recommend the right feature for production system. 6. Build predictive models to optimize business processes for a best in class customer experience. 7. Closely work with business owners and engineering teams to deploy new features and predictive models into production. 8. Visualize and report on project results. Minimum Requirements: 1. Minimum of 8 years’ related data science experience 2. Graduate degree in Statistics, Operations Research, Machine Learning or other related fields 3. Preferred Qualifications: 1. Proven ability and experience in using data science, statistical computing, and modeling to improve business KPIs. 2. Strong understanding of ecommerce and payments systems. 3. Ability to be flexible enough to jump in and act as a team-member, taking immediate ownership of unanticipated scenarios, and responding to ad hoc requests requiring database skill to solve. 4.
